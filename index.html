About Jaipur Metro

The Jaipur Metro Rail Corporation Ltd., abbreviated to JMRC, is a state-owned company[10] that operates the Jaipur Metro. The Jaipur Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. was created on 1 January 2010 BY MR AJAY GHOSH serving as the MANGING DIRECTOR.[11] Among the Rapid Transit systems of India, it has been recorded fastest to conduct of trial run after starting construction, when it commenced trial runs in Jaipur on 18 September 2013 flagged off by the then Chief Minister of Rajasthan Shri Ashok Gehlot.
